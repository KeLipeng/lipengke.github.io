    About Me

    I am currently a first year PhD student at State University of New York , CVML Lab, Computer Science Department, University at Albany, SUNY supervised by Prof. Siwei Lyu, and I also worked closely with Prof. Ming-Ching Chang. Before that I got my Master's Degree from University of Chinese Academy of Science in July 2018. 

    My research interests covers machine learning, deep learning and computer vision. Recently I focus on human pose estimation and object detection/segmentation.

    Updates

    (01/09/2019) Our paper Fast Online Video Pose Estimation by Dynamic Bayesian Modeling of Mode Transitions got accepted by TCYB2019.

    (07/03/2019) Our paper Multi-Scale Structure-Aware Network for Human Pose Estimation got accepted by ECCV2018.

    (05/04/2018) Our paper Multi-Scale Supervised Network for Human Pose Estimation got accepted as oral paper by ICIP2018.

    (04/02/2018) Our pose estimation work is ranked #1 on the very challenging MPII dataset, and the Video Demonstration is available.
